# Skin

> A loaded Rainmeter `.ini` together with everything it draws on screen.

In Monterey, each widget *size* file (`Widgets/Clock/Medium.ini`) loaded into Rainmeter
is one skin instance. The settings panel (`Settings.ini`) and each companion in
`Extras/` are also skins. A skin is built from [[Section|sections]] and assembled via the
[[Skin Composition Flow]].
