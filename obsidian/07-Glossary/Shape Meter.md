# Shape Meter

> A [[Meter]] of type `Shape` that draws vector primitives — rectangles, ellipses, lines
> — with fills, strokes, and rounded corners.

Shape meters are Monterey's main building block: widget backgrounds, the rounded
`Container` masks, clock hands, monitor gauges, and progress arcs are all shapes. A
single shape meter can stack multiple `Shape`, `Shape2`, … primitives and apply
transforms like rotation.
