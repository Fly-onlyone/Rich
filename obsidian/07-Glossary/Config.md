# Config

> A folder-and-`.ini` unit that Rainmeter can load, unload, and refresh independently.

Every `Widgets/<Name>/<Size>.ini`, every `Extras/*/*.ini`, and the root `Settings.ini`
is a separate config. Bangs such as `!ActivateConfig` and `!Refresh` operate on configs
by name. "Load a config" and "load a [[Skin]]" are used interchangeably.
